Home

Hi my name is Dylan Jepsen and I am a young geospatial professional specialising in surveying. I am passionate about how the geospatial science industry can provide solutions to modern day problems. This geoportfolio aims to display some of my recent work.

About

I have been working as a cadastral surveyor and geospatial professional with 3 years of industry experience. I am a passionate and dedicated team member with a willingness to learn and adapt to new tasks and environments. Through previous employment roles and volunteer positions I have developed a highly personable personality and effective communication skills. I have a love of the arts, particularly music and visuals.
